About Us

We are the official Australian gateway to TOP MODEL OF THE WORLD, one of the world's most prestigious modeling competitions. Our mission is spotlight rising stars who embody beauty, confidence, and individuallity. Join us in celebrating Australia's diverse talent and global potential.

THE INTERNATIONAL PAGEANT

Top Model of the World is structured as an annual international pageant with a format similar to major beauty contests, but with a stronger focus on modeling. Globally, the pageant spans around two weeks of events leading up to a grand finale.

The Experience

During this period, contestants from each participating country engage in photoshoots, video shoots, and themed fashion events. For instance, in the 31st edition (2025 in Egypt), the program included special theme nights such as a ’70s/’80s Party, Red Carpet Glamour Night, and a Black & White Dress Event in luxurious venues. These activities provide judges and media more opportunities to evaluate the contestants’ versatility and personality in different settings.

The Competition Show

The culmination is a final competition show where contestants are typically judged in several segments. According to the official judging criteria, each delegate is evaluated in three main areas:

  • Interview
  • Swimsuit
  • Evening Gown

These preliminary scores determine the overall winners—the contestant with the highest total becomes Top Model of the World, with runners-up named according to their ranking.

The Grand Finale

The finale often features a staged production with choreographed opening numbers, introductions of each country’s model, the segmented competitions, and an on-stage question or interview portion to assess personality and intelligence. The reigning titleholder then ceremonially crowns the new winner at the end of the night.
