About Us

We are the official Australian gateway to TOP MODEL OF THE WORLD, one of the world's most prestigious modeling competitions. Our mission is spotlight rising stars who embody beauty, confidence, and individuality. Join us in celebrating Australia's diverse talent and global potential.

THE INTERNATIONAL PAGEANT

Top Model of the World is structured as an annual international pageant with a format similar to major beauty contests, but with a stronger focus on modeling. Globally, the pageant spans around two weeks of events leading up to a grand finale.

The Experience

During this period, contestants from each participating country engage in photoshoots, video shoots, and themed fashion events. These activities provide judges and media more opportunities to evaluate the contestants’ versatility and personality in different settings.

The Competition Show

The culmination is a final competition show where contestants are typically judged in several segments. Each delegate is evaluated in three main areas:

Interview
Swimsuit
Evening Gown
